Nigerian Jollof Rice

A classic West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, peppers, and flavorful spices. Perfect for parties, gatherings, and everyday meals.

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 6 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 4 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1 cup blended tomatoes & peppers
  • 2 medium onions (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • Salt and seasoning cubes to taste
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pot, saut√© onions, then add tomato paste and fry for 3 minutes.
  3. Add blended tomatoes & peppers, seasonings, thyme, curry, and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Pour in chicken stock and bay leaf, stir well, then add the rice.
  5. Cover with foil, reduce heat, and steam until the rice is cooked and fluffy.

Tips

For smoky party-style Jollof, let the rice burn slightly at the bottom for that signature flavor.
